Channel Letter Types

We fabricate a full range of LED channel letters to match different architectural styles, budgets, and branding requirements.

Front-Lit Channel Letters

Bright, clean illumination through the acrylic face for maximum visibility.

Halo-Lit (Back-Lit) Channel Letters

Soft back-lighting creates a premium halo glow around each letter.

Metal Channel Letters

Neon Style Letters

Custom Logo Letters

Fully customized channel letters fabricated to match your brand identity.

Premium Fabrication Options

We offer a variety of styles to suit your building's architecture and brand requirements:

  • Front-lit channel letters: The standard for maximum brightness and visibility.
  • Back-lit (halo-lit) channel letters: Provides a sophisticated, glowing aura around letters.
  • Custom colors and finishes: Precision painting and acrylic options.
  • Multiple font styles: From modern sans-serifs to custom brand scripts.
  • Logo-based designs: We can replicate any complex logo shape.
