Our Services

Comprehensive lighting and signage solutions for commercial and retail properties.

1

Sign Design & Customization

Our creative team works with you on font selection, colors, and lighting styles to match your brand perfectly. We offer full custom logo and branding support for new and existing businesses.

2

Sign Fabrication

We build high-quality signage in-house, specializing in LED channel letters, durable cabinet and lightbox signs, and custom awning signage for retail storefronts.

3

Permit Assistance

We take the stress out of compliance. Our team prepares sign permit drawings and provides full coordination support with local municipalities to ensure your project meets all codes.

4

Licensed Electrical Services

As licensed electrical contractors, we provide dedicated circuits for signage, code-compliant electrical connections, and broad commercial electrical work for your entire facility.

5

Sign Installation & Maintenance

Professional installation using our fleet of bucket and crane trucks. We also provide ongoing repairs, LED retrofits, and preventative maintenance to keep your brand glowing.
