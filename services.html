Our Services

Our Services

LumSign provides full-service commercial signage solutions for businesses across British Columbia. From concept and fabrication to installation and ongoing maintenance, we deliver reliable, code-compliant, and visually impactful sign solutions.

LED Channel Letters

Custom-fabricated LED channel letters designed to enhance brand visibility day and night. Suitable for retail storefronts, office buildings, and commercial plazas.

  • Front-lit, back-lit, and halo-lit options
  • Energy-efficient LED modules
  • CSA-approved power supplies
  • Professional electrical installation

Lightbox & Sign Cabinet Retrofits

Upgrade outdated fluorescent or neon signage to modern LED systems. Improve brightness, reduce energy consumption, and lower maintenance costs.

  • Complete removal of ballasts and legacy components
  • Uniform illumination with no hot spots
  • Improved reliability and lifespan

Pylon & Freestanding Signs

Design, fabrication, and installation of pylon and freestanding signs for commercial properties, shopping centers, and industrial sites.

  • Single-tenant and multi-tenant pylons
  • Structural steel frameworks
  • Municipal sign permit coordination

Sign Installation & Electrical Work

Licensed electrical sign installation performed by certified professionals. All work complies with local electrical codes and safety regulations.

  • New sign installations
  • Electrical service connections
  • Disconnects, timers, and controls

Maintenance & Emergency Service

Ongoing sign maintenance and troubleshooting services to keep your signage operating reliably. Emergency service available when downtime matters.

  • LED module replacement
  • Power supply diagnostics
  • 24/7 emergency response
