About LumSign

Complete LED & Sign Solutions

LumSign provides complete LED lighting and sign solutions for commercial properties. We specialize in custom signage, LED fabrication, and licensed electrical installation.

As a licensed electrical contractor, we ensure all installations are safe, compliant, and professionally executed. Our one-stop approach covers everything from the initial design and permit coordination to final wiring and long-term maintenance.

Our team of certified electricians and sign technicians are equipped with the latest technology and specialized fleet to handle projects of any scale, from local retail storefronts to regional shopping centers.
